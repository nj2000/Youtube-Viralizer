Vocabulary library

Controls whether generated titles, hooks, and SEO copy reflect niche-specific phrasing.

Use niche vocabulary library

When on, Stage 5 and Stage 10 are softly biased toward AI tools / productivity language ("I tested", "no code", "in 2026"). Turn off if you want generic, brand-neutral output that doesn't lean into niche cliché.

Channel voice overrides library

When the library suggests phrasing that conflicts with your past titles or scripts, your voice wins. Recommended.

Sub-niche match priority

When your niche overlaps multiple sub-niches, prefer the most-specific match before falling back to broader vocabulary.
