Composition

  • Focal point: contrast between two icons + center headline
  • Subject: no face — type and symbol driven

Palette · click to copy

primary · neutral · contrast · accent

Facial expression

No face. (Optional secondary version: small inset of creator with a concerned, brow-furrowed expression looking down.)

Overlay text · 3 words

YOU'RE TOO LATE

Background

Hard 50/50 vertical split. Left desaturated gray with rising chart icon. Right deep crimson with a skull or warning symbol. No gradients on the divider — keep it abrupt.

type-driven before/after muted
Why it works

Loss aversion is sharper than gain. The before/after split short-circuits FOMO — viewers click to find out if they really are too late, and to see the disqualifying details.
