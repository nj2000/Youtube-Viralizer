Inline on /shorts/new

15s is too tight for 4 models × 3 categories

You'd need ~12 cuts at 1.25s each — viewers can't read overlay text that fast. Bump to 45s for a 11-cut version, or narrow to 2 models in 15s.

Heads up — multi-cut production budget

Scripts with 12+ cuts take ~3× the editing time. We'll offer a lower-cut variant in the output if cuts exceed budget.
